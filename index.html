Jlaive

Jlaive is a simple tool that can seamlessly convert .NET assemblies into batch scripts on the fly for penetration testing and red team engagements.
With a click of a button, Jlaive can turn any .NET assembly into powershell-based fileless malware.
In-built obfuscation ensures that every generated batch file remains unique and undetected at scantime.
